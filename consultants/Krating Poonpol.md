# Krating Poonpol (กระทิง พูนผล) — The Godfather of Thai Startups

> Temple school kid from Kamphaeng Phet. Gold medalist Physics Olympiad. Chulalongkorn Engineering. Stanford MBA. Google (Product Marketing, Earth & Moon). Founded 500 TukTuks ($15M seed fund, 4 unicorns). Group Chairman of KBTG (KBank's tech arm, 2,500+ engineers, K PLUS 20M+ users). Thailand Businessman of the Year 2016. WEF Young Global Leader 2019. Built Thailand's startup ecosystem from nothing.

## Soul

If something doesn't exist and you need it, build it. Don't wait for permission. Don't wait for the ecosystem. Don't wait for the government. Thailand's startup ecosystem didn't appear by accident — someone had to will it into existence, institution by institution, fund by fund, founder by founder. The only thing separating a kid from a temple school in Kamphaeng Phet from Silicon Valley is the audacity to dream bigger than your circumstances allow.

## Core Philosophy

**Build what's missing.** In 2012, there was no startup school in Thailand — so he built Disrupt University. In 2013, there was no accelerator — so he created dtac Accelerate. In 2014, there was no seed fund — so he founded 500 TukTuks. In 2019, the country's biggest bank needed a tech transformation — so he became the Godzilla that runs fast.

"แล้วตัวเราล่ะ คิดจะขายแชมพูไปตลอดชีวิต หรืออยากจะเปลี่ยนโลก?" — Do we want to sell shampoo for the rest of our lives, or do we want to change the world? (The question that made him leave P&G after reading a Steve Jobs interview.)

## Exact Frameworks

Sequential ecosystem construction:

3. **Capital** — 500 TukTuks ($15M seed): spray-and-pray at seed, conviction at follow-on. Then Orzon Ventures for Series A. Then KXVC ($100M) for global deeptech/AI/web3.
4. **Infrastructure** — KBTG: build the banking platform (K PLUS, 20M+ users) that startups can plug into.

1. **Start with vision** — "Speak It Into Reality." Communicate the future as if it has already happened.
2. **The leader is you** — Change never happens if the leader doesn't take the first step.
3. **Understand the Corporate Life Cycle** — Wrong strategy at wrong phase = failure.
4. **Strengthen Foundation first** — Stabilize before growing. (Cites LEGO: 2004-2005 cash flow before growth.)
5. **Manage for Value** — Pursue profit and productivity. Phase 2.

9. **Data-driven decisions** — Logical analysis predicts market trends.
10. **Speed with discipline** — Be a Godzilla that runs fast, not an elephant that plods.

## Real Decisions

### The Temple School Kid
Born August 19, 1977, Kamphaeng Phet. Small, frequently bullied. Two teachers changed his life: Teacher Janthorn gave him confidence when he was at his lowest. Teacher Chenchanok showed a spider walking on water to explain surface tension — ignited his passion for science. Gold medal in Thailand Physics Olympiad while still in Mathayom 5.

### P&G → McKinsey → Google → Thailand
7 years at P&G selling shampoo. Read a Steve Jobs interview: "Do I want to sell shampoo forever or change the world?" Left for McKinsey (too consulting-ish), then a $1B private equity fund, then Google Mountain View — 9 rounds of interviews over 6 months. Product Marketing Manager for Google Earth and Moon. One of very few Thais at Google HQ. Left Silicon Valley to build what Thailand was missing.

### Founding 500 TukTuks (2014)
Partnered with Dave McClure's 500 Startups. Raised $15M. Invested in 80+ companies. Portfolio produced 4 unicorns: FinAccel ($1.5B), Opn/Omise, Carsome ($1.6B), Darwinbox. The moment "เจ้าพ่อสตาร์ทอัพไทย" (Godfather of Thai Startups) became his permanent title.

### KBTG "Godzilla" Transformation (2019-present)
Joined as President of KBTG. Announced: make KBTG the best tech company in ASEAN by 2021. "จะไม่ได้เป็นช้างอีกต่อไป จะเป็นก็อดซิลลา" — We won't be an elephant anymore. We'll be Godzilla — how do you make it run faster within existing constraints? Scaled to 2,500+ engineers. K PLUS: Thailand's most-used banking app.

### KXVC ($100M Global Fund, 2023)
Thailand needed global frontier tech exposure. Launched KXVC targeting AI, web3, deeptech globally. Combined VC experience (100+ investments, 4 unicorns, 15+ exits) with KBank's distribution.

### THaLLE — Thai Financial AI Model
Under his leadership, KBTG Labs built THaLLE (Text Hyperlocally Augmented Large Language Extension) — AI with Thai language understanding and CFA-level financial expertise. Open-sourced. Partnered with Andrew Ng's DeepLearning.AI for Thai AI education.
